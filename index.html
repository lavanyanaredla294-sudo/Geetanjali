About Geetanjali School

Geetanjali School has served the Nambur community for over 25 years. We focus on creating confident, curious learners through joyful classroom activities, reading programs, and value-based education.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To empower every child with quality education, values, and confidence to succeed in the modern world.

Mission:

  • Provide joyful, activity-based learning.
  • Strengthen English and maths foundation using phonics and games.
  • Use appropriate technology to enhance learning.
  • Partner with parents for holistic development.

Academics

  • Phonics-based English for early grades
  • Activity based maths and foundational numeracy
  • Hands-on science and exploration
  • Basic computer literacy

Activities

  • Phonics & Reading Club
  • Math Games & Problem Solving
  • Science Experiments
  • Art & Craft
  • Computer Basics
